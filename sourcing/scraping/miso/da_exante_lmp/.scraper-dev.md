# MISO Day-Ahead Ex-Ante LMP Scraper

Collects Day-Ahead Ex-Ante Locational Marginal Pricing data from MISO's public market reports portal.

## Data Source
- **URL Pattern**: https://docs.misoenergy.org/marketreports/{YYYYMMDD}_da_exante_lmp.csv
- **Format**: CSV
- **Update**: Daily (one file per day)
- **Historical**: Yes - date-based URLs
- **Authentication**: None

## What is Day-Ahead Ex-Ante LMP?

Day-Ahead Ex-Ante LMP represents forecasted Locational Marginal Prices for the day-ahead market. Published before the operating day, these prices reflect the expected cost of delivering electricity to specific locations in the MISO grid.
